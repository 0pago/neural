Interactive exploration of neural-network models

As part of the AToM US SciDAC project two neural-network (NN) based models were developed: EPED1-NN for the prediction of the H-mode pedestal structure; and TGLF-NN for the prediction of core turbulent transport fluxes. These neural-network models were trained to reproduce the results of the original TGLF and EPED1 theory-based models over a broad range of plasma regimes.

The resulting EPED1-NN and TGLF-NN models are 9 and 5 orders of magnitude faster than running EPED1 and TGLF directly. The following web-applications allow the interactive exploration of these neural-network based models. The details of the implementations of both EPED1-NN and TGLF-NN can be found in Ref. [Meneghini et al. IAEA 2017].

EPED1-NN - H-mode pedestal structure

A neural-network based regression of the EPED1 model [Snyder et al. PoP 2009].

TGLF-NN - Core turbulent transport fluxes

A neural-network based regression of the TGLF model [Staebler et al. PoP 2007].
