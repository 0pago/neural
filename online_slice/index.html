Interactive exploration of neural-network models

The following web-applications allow the interactive exploration of the EPED1-NN and TGLF-NN neural-network based models for the prediction of the H-mode plasma pedestal structure and of core turbulent transport fluxes (particle, energy, and momentum). The details of the implementations of these models can be found in Ref. [Meneghini IAEA 2017].

EPED1-NN - for the prediction of the H-mode plasma pedestal structure

A neural-network based regression of the EPED1 model [Snyder PoP 2009].

TGLF-NN - for the prediction of core turbulent transport fluxes

A neural-network based regression of the TGLF model [Staebler PoP 2007].
